# Project Blueprint: Local WAV to MP3 Converter (AdSense Optimized)

## Overview
A secure, client-side WAV to MP3 converter. This project is specifically optimized for Google AdSense approval with high-value editorial content, comprehensive legal pages, and professional SEO. 

To resolve the Google AdSense "Low-Value Content" (가치가 별로 없는 콘텐츠) rejection, we have transformed the site into a professional **Audio Engineering Knowledge Base & Converter Hub**. We have expanded the page count from 5 to 15 indexable URLs by launching 10 highly detailed, standalone audio-tech guides, and refactoring `guides.html` into a beautiful central blog-style directory.

## Features
- **Privacy-First:** 100% client-side WAV to MP3 conversion using LameJS. No server uploads.
- **High Quality:** Supports bitrates up to 320kbps.
- **Modern UI:** Built with Tailwind CSS, featuring dark/light mode aesthetics and responsive design.
- **Audio Encyclopedia (10 Standalone Guides):** Over 10,000 words of deeply researched original Korean text across 10 standalone articles to meet AdSense minimum content & structural requirements.
- **Technical SEO:** Optimized meta tags, unique titles, and semantic HTML for superior search engine crawling.
- **Unified Navigation & Analytics:** Consistent header, footer, Google Tag Manager, Analytics, and Microsoft Clarity tags on all 15 pages.
