# Project Blueprint: Local WAV to MP3 Converter (AdSense Authority Edition)

## Overview
A secure, client-side WAV to MP3 converter. This project is evolved from a simple tool into a professional **Audio Engineering Knowledge Hub** to satisfy Google AdSense's "High-Value Content" and E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) requirements.

The goal is to move beyond "sufficient content" to "authoritative content" by deepening technical insights, establishing a professional persona, and providing a comprehensive user experience.

## Core Strategy for AdSense Approval (Low-Value Content Fix)
To resolve the "Low-Value Content" rejection, we are implementing a four-pillar authority strategy:
1. **Deep Content (Authority):** Transforming introductory guides into "Deep-Dive" articles with expert tips, case studies, and technical nuances.
2. **Trust Signals (Trustworthiness):** Adding critical legal and technical pages (Terms of Service, Technical Glossary) to signal professional operations.
3. **E-E-A-T Persona (Expertise):** Shifting from a generic "tool" voice to a "Professional Audio Engineering Team" persona.
4. **Knowledge Graph (UX):** Implementing a robust internal linking structure (Related Articles, Glossary links) to increase session duration and perceived value.

## Features
- **Privacy-First:** 100% client-side WAV to MP3 conversion.
- **High Quality:** Supports bitrates up to 320kbps.
- **Modern UI:** Professional dark/light mode aesthetics using Tailwind CSS.
- **Audio Encyclopedia:** 10+ deeply researched guides + a comprehensive Technical Glossary.
- **Trust Infrastructure:** Comprehensive Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and professional About page.
